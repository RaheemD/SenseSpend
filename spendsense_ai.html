Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your data

1. Introduction

Welcome to SpendSense AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your financial data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our expense tracking and budgeting application.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials (password or Google account information).

Financial Data: We collect and store the financial information you provide, including expenses, income, budgets, savings goals, receipt images, and transaction details.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our app, including features used, chat messages with our AI assistant, and app performance metrics.

Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system, browser type, and IP address for security and optimization purposes.

3. How We Store Your Data

Cloud Storage: When you sign in, your data is securely stored in Google Firebase Cloud Firestore, a secure and encrypted cloud database service.

Local Storage: If you use the app in offline mode without signing in, your data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Data Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols. Data at rest is encrypted using Firebase's built-in encryption.

4. How We Use Your Data

Service Provision: We use your data to provide expense tracking, budgeting, AI-powered insights, receipt scanning (OCR), and financial coaching features.

AI Processing: Your financial data and chat messages are processed by OpenRouter API (using Google Gemini 3 Flash model) to provide personalized financial insights, spending analysis, and conversational assistance.

Improvement: We analyze aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to improve our app features and user experience.

Communication: We use your email to send account-related notifications, support responses, and important service updates.

5. Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for authentication, database storage, and hosting. Firebase's privacy policy applies: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

OpenRouter API: We use OpenRouter to access AI models for financial insights and chat features. Your financial data is processed through their API but is not stored by OpenRouter beyond the request lifecycle.

Netlify: Our app is hosted on Netlify. Support form submissions are processed through Netlify Forms.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
  • With service providers who assist in operating our app (Firebase, OpenRouter) under strict confidentiality agreements
7. Your Rights and Choices

Access: You can access all your data at any time through the app interface.

Export: You can download your expense data as a CSV file using the "Download CSV" feature in Settings.

Deletion: You can delete specific expenses, budgets, or goals at any time. To delete all your data, use the "Wipe Ledger" button in Settings.

Account Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data using the "Delete Account" button in Settings. This action is irreversible.

Offline Mode: You can use the app without creating an account by choosing "Skip for now" during sign-in. Your data will remain local to your device.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, all your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes.

9. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Encrypted data storage using Firebase security
  • Secure authentication with Firebase Auth
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Optional biometric lock screen for app access

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

SpendSense AI is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using SpendSense AI, you consent to the transfer of your data to our servers and third-party service providers.

12. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

For EU Users (GDPR): You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

For California Users (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, delete your personal information, and opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data).

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

Email: privacy@spendsense.ai
Support Form: Use the Customer Support form in Settings

Disclaimer: SpendSense AI is a personal finance management tool designed for budgeting and expense tracking. It does not provide professional financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. All AI-generated insights and recommendations are for informational purposes only. Consult with qualified professionals for specific financial advice.
